Quantum Mechanics Simulator

Tags: Physics · Numerical Methods

This educational tool simulates one‑dimensional quantum wave functions in various potential wells. By numerically solving the Schrödinger equation, the simulator visualises probability densities, energy levels and time evolution to provide intuition about quantum systems.
